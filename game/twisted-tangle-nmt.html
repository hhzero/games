Twisted Tangle

Untangle the lines and boost your logical thinking skills!

Game Overview

Twisted Tangle is an engaging puzzle game that challenges players to untangle complex interwoven lines. Your goal is to rearrange different nodes so that none of the connecting lines cross each other.

While the game concept seems simple, it offers a significant challenge that effectively exercises your spatial thinking and logical reasoning skills. As you progress through the levels, the puzzles become increasingly difficult, providing continuous challenges and satisfaction.

How to Play

Move Nodes

Click and drag the circular nodes to move them. Your goal is to rearrange the nodes so that none of the connecting lines cross.

Observe Connections

Carefully observe how the lines are connected and identify which nodes can be moved to reduce crossing points.

Limited Moves

Some levels have a limited number of moves. Plan your moves carefully to complete the puzzle within the given constraints.

Use Hints

If you get stuck, use the hint feature. Hints will show possible next moves to help you progress.

Game Features

  • Over 100 carefully designed levels with increasing difficulty
  • Clean and intuitive interface with easy-to-understand controls
  • Relaxing background music that enhances the puzzle-solving experience
  • Casual mode without time limits, allowing you to think at your own pace
  • Progress saving functionality to continue your puzzle journey anytime
  • Beautiful visuals and smooth animations

Tips for Success

Start with the edge nodes, as they're usually easier to position correctly. Try moving connected nodes as groups, as sometimes moving a cluster of nodes can solve multiple crossing issues at once.
