Twisted Tangle

Untangle the knots and sharpen your brain in this addictive puzzle game!

Game Overview

Twisted Tangle is a brain-teasing puzzle game that challenges you to untangle a web of intersecting lines. Your mission is simple yet tricky: rearrange the nodes so none of the connecting lines cross each other.

Though the concept seems straightforward, the game offers a genuine mental workout that boosts your spatial awareness and problem-solving skills. Each level gets progressively more challenging, keeping you engaged and delivering that satisfying "aha!" moment when you solve a particularly tricky puzzle.

How to Play

Drag to Move

Simply click and drag the circular nodes to reposition them. Your goal is to arrange them so no lines cross over each other.

Study the Pattern

Take a moment to analyze how everything connects. Look for nodes that, when moved, would eliminate multiple crossings at once.

Mind Your Moves

Some levels restrict how many moves you can make. Plan carefully to solve the puzzle within these constraints.

Stuck? Get Help

If you're stumped, tap the hint button. It'll highlight potential moves to help you make progress.

Game Features

  • Over 100 cleverly designed puzzles with increasing complexity
  • Sleek, minimalist interface with intuitive controls
  • Soothing soundtrack that enhances your puzzle-solving experience
  • No time pressure – solve at your own pace
  • Auto-save feature that remembers your progress
  • Crisp visuals with satisfying animations

Pro Tips

Begin with the outer nodes – they're typically easier to position correctly. Try moving connected nodes as a group; sometimes shifting several at once solves multiple crossings. If you hit a wall, don't be afraid to start fresh – a clean slate often helps you see new solutions. And remember, the best puzzlers know that patience beats frustration every time!
