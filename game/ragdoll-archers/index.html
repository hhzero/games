Ragdoll Archers

Master the bow and arrow in this wacky physics-based battle game!

Game Overview

Ragdoll Archers is a hilarious physics-based archery game that pits you against opponents in wild bow-and-arrow showdowns. With realistic ragdoll physics, each arrow shot creates unpredictable and often comical results as you take down your foes.

Whether you're facing off against computer opponents or challenging friends in local multiplayer, Ragdoll Archers offers easy-to-learn controls with surprising tactical depth that'll keep you hooked for hours.

How to Play

Take Aim

Click and drag to pull back your bow. The further you pull, the more power and distance your shot will have.

Fire Away

Release to let your arrow fly. Factor in wind, gravity, and distance when targeting your opponents.

Position Strategically

Use the terrain to gain high ground or find cover. Smart positioning can make all the difference in battle.

Outsmart Enemies

Go for direct hits or try trick shots to take out multiple opponents. Adapt your tactics to each unique battlefield.

Game Features

  • Hilarious ragdoll physics that create unpredictable and laugh-out-loud moments
  • Multiple game modes including campaign, survival challenges, and local multiplayer
  • Diverse battlefields with unique environmental obstacles and advantages
  • Unlock new archer characters and weapon upgrades as you progress
  • Pick-up-and-play controls that are intuitive yet offer room for mastery
