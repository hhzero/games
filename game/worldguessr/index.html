WorldGuessr

Put your geography skills to the test and discover amazing places around the world!

Game Overview

WorldGuessr is an addictive geography game that challenges you to figure out where in the world you've been dropped based on street view images. It's a free platform where geography buffs can test their knowledge while virtually exploring fascinating locations across the globe.

From urban metropolises to quiet country backroads, your surroundings are clues to solve, making each round an exciting geographical treasure hunt. Whether you're a student, teacher, or just someone who loves adventure, WorldGuessr delivers an experience you'll love.

How to Play

Explore

The game drops you somewhere in the world at random. Use your mouse to look around and navigate the area to gather clues.

Find Clues

Look for landmarks, road signs, architectural styles, and local languages to help pinpoint which region or country you're in.

Make a Guess

Once you have a hunch about your location, click that spot on the map. Your score depends on how close your guess is to the actual location.

Need Help?

If you're completely lost, you can use hints that reveal your general area, like the continent or region you're in.

Game Features

  • Endless rounds to keep you exploring locations worldwide
  • Diverse settings from big cities to remote rural areas
  • Challenge friends or players globally in multiplayer mode
  • User-friendly interface that works for players of all ages
  • Learn while having fun – improve your geography knowledge through play

Pro Tips

Watch which side of the road cars are driving on – this can tell you if you're in a left-driving or right-driving country. Scan street signs and ads for language clues. The architecture, plant life, and climate can also give away your location. Most importantly, keep playing! The more you practice, the better you'll get at recognizing where in the world you are.
