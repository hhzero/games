Mahjongg Solitaire

Find matching pairs in this addictive tile-clearing classic!

Game Overview

Mahjongg Solitaire puts a puzzle spin on the ancient Chinese game of Mahjong. The goal couldn't be simpler: pair up identical tiles to clear them from the board until you've removed every last one. This digital version brings all the classic satisfaction with eye-catching visuals and smooth gameplay.

With dozens of intricate layouts to tackle and various difficulty settings, Mahjongg Solitaire offers endless brain-teasing fun. The zen-like pace and satisfying click of matching tiles make it perfect for quick gaming breaks or longer sessions of puzzle-solving bliss.

How to Play

Spot & Match

Click on matching pairs of free tiles to remove them from the board. You can only select tiles that aren't blocked on both sides by other tiles.

Find "Free" Tiles

A tile is "free" when its left or right edge isn't touching another tile. You'll need to clear top-layer tiles before accessing those underneath.

Think Ahead

Don't just click the first matches you see! Sometimes it's smarter to leave certain pairs for later to unblock crucial tiles underneath.

Beat the Clock

Some levels feature timers. Work efficiently to match all tiles before time runs out to earn bonus points and special rewards.

Pro Tips

Always work from the top down – clear the highest tiles first to access those buried underneath. Focus on removing tiles that are blocking the most other pieces. Train your eyes to spot matching pairs across the entire board – it's easy to miss matches that are far apart. If you're stuck, use a hint, but try not to rely on them too much (that's where the real satisfaction comes from!). Most importantly, be patient and think a few moves ahead – sometimes it's better to ignore an obvious pair if removing it would leave you with no future options.
